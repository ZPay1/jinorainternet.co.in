Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Jinorainternet Private Limited ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our services and visit our platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device by websites, applications, or advertisements. These files help remember your preferences and improve your overall experience.

Why We Use Cookies
  • Authenticate and secure user sessions
  • Store user preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage trends
  • Deliver and measure the effectiveness of advertising
  • Understand user behavior and improve user experience
Other Tracking Technologies

We may also use web beacons (pixel tags or clear GIFs), mobile device identifiers, flash cookies, and similar technologies. These help us measure interactions, email open rates, or ad engagement.

Types of Cookies We Use

Some cookies are essential for website functionality, while others are optional and used for analytics or marketing purposes.

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functions (e.g., login sessions)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand site usage to improve user experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Used by third-party services to deliver targeted advertising
Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted partners to use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. These partners may collect data about your online activities across different websites.

Consent

By continuing to use our platform with cookies enabled in your browser, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
